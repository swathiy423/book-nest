Questions

Before you join

Is BookNest free?+
Yes — the library, reading rooms, clubs, and marketplace are all free. We may introduce optional perks later, but the core experience stays free.
How does the book exchange work?+
List a book, get matched with someone nearby who wants it, message them to agree on a swap, then confirm in person with a QR code.
Can I import my Goodreads shelves?+
Yes, upload your Goodreads export CSV during onboarding and your shelves, ratings, and reviews carry straight over.
Is my exchange address shared publicly?+
No — location is only ever shown as an approximate radius until you and a match agree to meet.
